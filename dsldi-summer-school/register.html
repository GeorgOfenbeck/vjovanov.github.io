Summer School on DSL Design and Implementation

13th–18th July, Lausanne, Switzerland

Enrollment Form

Summer school registration is open to students, researchers, and experts in the area. There is an enrollment fee of 270 CHF for students. This fee covers accommodation, food, and a banquet at the end of the event. Note that we can finance only a limited number of international students so, based on the motivation letter and the CV, we will make the selection.

Non-student participants and non-financed students can still attend the school, however, they must cover the expenses of accommodation and food (approximately 800 CHF). Please contact us if you need additional information regarding affordable accommodation.
